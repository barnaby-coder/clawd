# EQ Journal Prompts

Rotate through these categories. Goal: build emotional awareness, self-regulation, empathy, and social skills.

## Self-Awareness (Weeks 1-2)
1. What emotion showed up strongest today? Where did you feel it in your body?
2. What moment today triggered the biggest emotional reaction in you? What was underneath that reaction?
3. When did you feel most like yourself today? When did you feel least like yourself?
4. What story were you telling yourself today that might not be fully true?
5. If you had to name your emotional weather today (sunny, stormy, foggy, etc.), what would it be and why?
6. What drained your energy today? What recharged it?
7. What's something you avoided today? What feeling was behind the avoidance?

## Self-Regulation (Weeks 3-4)
8. Describe a moment today where you paused before reacting. What happened because you paused?
9. When did you feel frustration or impatience today? How did you handle it vs how you wish you'd handled it?
10. What's one thing you wanted to say today but held back? Was holding back the right call?
11. When did stress peak for you today? What was your first impulse, and what did you actually do?
12. Think of a decision you made today. How much was logic vs emotion driving it?
13. What boundary did you set or wish you'd set today?
14. What's a pattern you noticed in how you react when things don't go as planned?

## Empathy & Social Awareness (Weeks 5-6)
15. Who did you interact with today that seemed to be carrying something heavy? How could you tell?
16. Think of a conversation today. What do you think the other person was really feeling underneath their words?
17. When did you feel most connected to someone today? What made that moment work?
18. Was there a moment today where you misread someone's emotion or intention? What happened?
19. Who on your team might need something from you that they haven't asked for?
20. What's something you assumed about someone today? Could you be wrong?
21. Think of a difficult person you dealt with today. What might their world look like from the inside?

## Relationship & Leadership (Weeks 7-8)
22. How did you make someone feel today? Was that intentional?
23. What conversation are you avoiding? What would it take to have it?
24. When did you lead with authority today vs lead with influence? Which worked better?
25. Who did you appreciate today but didn't tell? Why not?
26. Think of a conflict or tension. What's your role in keeping it alive?
27. How did you show up as a leader today? How did you show up as a human?
28. What would your team say about your energy today if they were honest?

## Integration (Ongoing)
29. What did you learn about yourself this week that surprised you?
30. What emotional skill feels stronger now than a month ago?
31. What's one thing you'd do differently if you could replay today?
32. Write a letter to yourself from 6 months in the future. What would that version of you say?
