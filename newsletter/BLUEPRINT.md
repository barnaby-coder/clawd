## Risk Mitigation

- **Time creep:** Hard cap at 30 min/week for Finneigan. If it takes more, the process needs fixing, not more effort.
- **Quality vs quantity:** One great edition beats two mediocre ones. Skip a week if needed rather than ship garbage.
- **Voice drift:** The opening insight must always be Finneigan's perspective. I draft, you make it yours. If it ever sounds generic, we've lost the moat.
- **Burnout:** This should feel energizing, not obligatory. If it becomes a chore, we redesign or pause.
